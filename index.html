Process Flow & Happy Customers

We begin by conducting a detailed assessment of the client's financial needs, understanding their business model and funding requirements. This allows us to tailor our reports to match the exact nature of the loan or credit facility being sought.

Our team works closely with the client to gather all necessary financial data, including historical financial statements, business plans, and projections. Our team analyses the financial data thoroughly to identify key metrics and risk factors that banks focus on during the approval process.

Leveraging our 12+ years of experience, we prepare precise and well-structured reports. Our reports are not generated by software but are manually crafted to ensure they reflect the intricacies of each unique business case. This approach guarantees higher acceptance rates from banks.

Our reports are designed in strict adherence to the criteria and standards established by banks for term loans and cash credit. This ensures that our reports seamlessly fit into the bank’s internal evaluation process & minimize the chances of report rejection or delays in loan processing.

Throughout the entire process, we maintain continuous communication with both the client and the bank, acting as a liaison to address any questions or concerns from either side. This level of coordination helps to avoid miscommunication and accelerates the loan process.

Once the report is completed, we assist in the submission to the bank. We ensure that any additional documentation or clarifications requested by the bank are promptly provided, further ensuring a smooth process.

Our service doesn’t end once the report is submitted. We continue to support the client post-approval, assisting with disbursement of funds, compliance with loan conditions, and monitoring for ongoing reporting requirements to the bank.
